Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the calculator?

Simply enter the number of pizzas you want to make, the desired weight per pizza, and the hydration percentage. The app will calculate all the ingredients for you.

What's the difference between the dough types?

Regular: Standard pizza dough with direct fermentation. Perfect for beginners and quick pizza making.
Poolish: A wet pre-ferment that adds complex flavor and improved texture. Requires 12-16 hours of fermentation.
Biga: A firm pre-ferment that adds structure and depth of flavor. Requires 12-24 hours of fermentation.

What hydration percentage should I use?

Regular dough: 60-65% is common for a balanced texture
Poolish: 100% hydration (equal parts water and flour)
Biga: 50-60% hydration for a firm consistency

How accurate are the calculations?

The app uses standard pizza dough ratios and formulas used by professional pizzaiolos. The calculations are precise and have been tested extensively.
